Risk Management and Diversification Principles

Historical Average Annual Returns (nominal, US):
- S&P 500: ~10-11%
- US small-cap: ~12% (higher volatility)
- International developed: ~8-9%
- US aggregate bonds: ~5-6%
- Cash/T-bills: ~3-4%
- Inflation: ~3%

Diversification: Hold broad index funds for instant diversification. Diversify across asset classes, geographies, and sectors. Correlation matters — uncorrelated assets improve portfolio efficiency.

Crypto: Highly volatile with limited history. Not reliably uncorrelated during crises. Most advisors recommend 0-5% max for moderate investors. Treat as speculative. Evolving regulatory environment.

Emergency Fund: 3-6 months essential expenses in high-yield savings or money market. Must be in place before aggressive investing — prevents forced selling during downturns.
